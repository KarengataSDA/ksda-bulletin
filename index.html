Thought for the Day

The life of Joseph mirrors Christ's life. His brothers sold him into slavery to prevent his rise, thinking they'd thwart his dreams. Yet, God used their actions to fulfill His plans, giving Joseph dominion over them. Joseph's self-control, patience, and unwavering faith, even in unjust suffering, serve as an enduring example for all.

OUR BELIEFS

Seventh-day Adventists accept the Bible as the only source of our beliefs. We consider our movement to be the result of the Protestant conviction that the Bible is the only standard of faith and practice for Christians.

Currently, Adventists hold 28 fundamental beliefs. To find out more visit our website www.karengatasda.org. Feel free to contact anyone of the pastoral staff too.
